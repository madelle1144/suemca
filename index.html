Susan Lumumba

MCA Candidate for Dandora 4/5 Ward

With over 10 years of community service experience, I am committed to bringing positive change to our ward. Throughout the years, I have worked closely with youth groups, local businesses, and development initiatives to improve livelihoods and create opportunities for growth.
My leadership approach is rooted in transparency, teamwork, and genuine concern for the people.
I believe in building solutions that empower every resident from supporting small enterprises to improving access to education and social welfare. Together, we can make our ward a model of unity, progress, and accountability.

About Me

My Journey to Public Service

I was born and raised in this community, and I've witnessed both its challenges and its incredible potential. After completing my education in Public Administration, I returned home with a mission to serve the people who shaped me.

For the past decade, I've worked tirelessly on various community development projects, from improving local schools to advocating for better healthcare facilities. These experiences have prepared me for the role of MCA, where I can amplify our community's voice at the county level.

My Ambition as MCA

As your MCA, my primary focus will be on improving infrastructure, enhancing healthcare services, and creating economic opportunities for our youth. I believe in a collaborative approach to governance, where community input directly shapes policy decisions.

My vision includes establishing vocational training centers, improving road networks, and ensuring transparent allocation of county resources to benefit every resident of West Ward.
